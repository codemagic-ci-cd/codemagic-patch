# Pricing

Self-host Codemagic Patch for free while you stay at or below 1,000,000 Monthly Active Users (unique devices that request an update in a calendar month). Above that, you need a commercial license from Codemagic.

The React Native SDK and CLI are [Apache 2.0](https://github.com/codemagic-ci-cd/codemagic-patch/blob/main/client/LICENSE). You can use them in your apps either way.

## Commercial rates

Above 1M MAU, pricing is per million users per year. Bands stack: each slice of MAU is billed at that band's rate.

| Monthly Active Users | Price (per million users per year) |
| --- | --- |
| 1-50 million | $990 |
| 50-100 million | $750 |
| Over 100 million | $500 |

Example: 120 million MAU is $97,000/year (50 × $990 + 50 × $750 + 20 × $500).

Commercial licenses include a dedicated Slack channel with Codemagic for support.

Codemagic can also host Patch for your company on request, for an additional cost.

To buy a license or talk about hosting, email [sales@codemagic.io](mailto:sales@codemagic.io).
